Step 2 of 25 Steps:
Community Stakeholder Interviews

In this step we come to your community and visit with as many of your stakeholders as possible in what we like to call "cup of coffee" visits. These are casual in nature to make people feel comfortable. We like to ask, "If we are sitting in this same room 3 years from now, what results do you want to have happened for you to look us in the eyes and thank us for finding you the right hire?"

Our Risk Reduction Measures

No search firm goes as far to reduce your risk as Next Move Group. How do we make such a claim?

  • We conduct credit and criminal background checks on everyone you interview. Our competitors typically only conduct these background checks on your finalist.
